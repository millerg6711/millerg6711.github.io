Why Choose Technical Education?

Today, too many students graduate with big debt, but limited career prospects. Career and technical education is different.

Graduates from high-quality technical colleges don’t struggle to find jobs in their fields – they’re in high demand.

  • Higher starting salaries
  • More job opportunities to pick from
  • Less lag time from graduation to employment
  • Great long-term potential
  • Earning up to $40,000, $50,000 or even $60,000 per year

Why Choose Ranken?

Want to land a successful, well-paying career now? Or, looking to quickly advance in your current job? Ranken Technical College may be the school for you.

At Ranken, you don’t just graduate with an education – you graduate ready to work. With a fast degree or certificate from Ranken, you can start your career in as little as two semesters.

We’re focused on you and how we can prepare you to succeed at every level. With our commitment to hands-on training, programs for only the most relevant, in-demand careers, and strong relationships with employers, we’re proud to be one of the best technical schools in the Midwest.

Here are just a few of the reasons you should consider attending Ranken Technical College.

  • Land the job you want
  • Be prepared for the real world.
  • Get an education that works
  • Find your passion
