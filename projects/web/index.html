About the Event

Run America’s Longest Main Street

Run a portion of America’s longest Main Street during this marathon only race held on a Saturday in historic Belleville, IL, near downtown St. Louis. The course also includes paved trails and is a Boston Marathon qualifier with enthusiastic crowd support.

All finishers will receive a medal, and all participants will receive a long-sleeve T-shirt among other goodies. Race amenities include ample water and fueling stations along the course, awards in numerous ivisions, energetic volunteers and spectators, and free post-race food, beverages, and entertainment provided by local restaurants and businesses.

We are a race by runners for runners. See what participants of the 2016 inaugural Belleville Main Street Marathon had to say:
