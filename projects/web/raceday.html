Race Day Information

COURSE:

The marathon will start at 7 a.m. around Main and First Streets in downtown Belleville, and course support will be available until 2 p.m. The course includes a portion of America’s longest Main Street, among other streets, and a paved trail.

LATE PACKET PICKUP AND REGISTRATION:

Packet pickup and on-site race-day registration will be available near the start/finish line around south 1st and Main streets from 5:30 to 6:30 a.m. on race morning.

RESTROOMS:

January 1 through August 15, registration is $65. Registration after August 15 is $75 and after September 15 is $85. A printed registration form and fee may also be mailed. Please make check payable to Belleville Running Club and mail to Toolen’s Running Start, Belleville Main Street Marathon, 3260 Green Mount Crossing Dr, Shiloh, IL 62269. On-site race day registration 5:30 to 6:30 a.m. near West Main and First streets in downtown Belleville. Entry fees are nonrefundable.

MEDICAL:

The top 3 overall winners and the top 3 finishers in each division (see below) will receive awards. All finishers will receive participant medals.

BONUS awards for the 2018 race only: RRCA Championship medals will be awarded to the top overall, master, grandmaster, and senior grandmaster male and female winners!

To recognize those who run the Belleville Main Street Marathon for consecutive years, we will present “The Streaker Award.” You must be fully clothed to receive this honor, which will be presented at the awards ceremony (must be present to receive award).

FINISHER MEDALS/AWARDS:

All race finishers will receive a medal at the finish line. Awards for the top 3 male and female, male and female Master’s, and male and female wheelchair finishers will be presented at 12 p.m. in the marathon plaza area just east of the finish line. Age group awards will be presented around 2 p.m. in the marathon plaza area. Awards will not be mailed but can be picked up at Toolen’s Running Start after October 3.

THE STREAKER AWARD:

Make the Belleville Main Street Marathon a tradition! All finishers who run the Belleville Marathon for consecutive years will be recognized with our commemorative Streaker Award. You must be fully clothed to receive this honor, which will be presented upon race completion at a marked table in the marathon plaza area just east of the finish line.

PACKET PICKUP:

Packet pickup will take place Friday, September 28 from 11 a.m. to 7 p.m. at Toolen’s Running Start, 3260 Green Mount Crossing Drive, Shiloh, IL 62269.

HYDRATION:

Water and lemon-lime Gatorade will be available approximately every 1 to 2 miles.

Gatorade Endurance Formula is the exclusive sports beverage of the Belleville Main Street Marathon.

ENERGY GEL:

GU stations will be located near miles 12, 18, and 21.

PARKING:

All participants/volunteers/spectators are strongly encouraged to park in the FREE St. Clair County parking garage, located at 1 Lincoln Street and conveniently very close to the start/finish line. The garage will be monitored periodically by race officials.

POST-RACE REFRSHMENTS:

After the race, hang around, relax, refuel, and celebrate your finish with award-winning, nationally acclaimed barbecue and fixins’ donated by Belleville’s very own BEAST Craft BBQ, and Michelob Ultra, donated by Belleville’s Grey Eagle Distributors. Yum!

RACE-DAY PHOTOS:

Professional photos will be available for purchase and may be viewed at bellevillemarathonphotos.rutster.com.
